Meet the Characters

Explore the vibrant and diverse characters from the How to Train Your Dragon universe. Each character brings a unique personality and dragon partner to the story, making every adventure unforgettable.

Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III

Hiccup is the inventive and kind-hearted son of Stoick the Vast. Although initially an outcast, he becomes the first Viking to befriend a dragon—Toothless—and eventually becomes the chief of Berk.

Astrid Hofferson

Astrid is a fierce and determined warrior known for her strength and loyalty. She is Hiccup's closest confidante and eventual wife, always standing by his side through every battle.

Stoick the Vast

Stoick is the proud and powerful chief of Berk. Despite initial skepticism about dragons, he eventually supports Hiccup's vision of a peaceful coexistence between Vikings and dragons.

Snotlout Jorgenson

Snotlout is a brash and competitive Viking who rides the aggressive Monstrous Nightmare named Hookfang. Although boastful, he proves himself a brave and valuable warrior.

Fishlegs Ingerman

Fishlegs is an intelligent and dragon-obsessed Viking who rides Meatlug, a gentle Gronckle. His knowledge about dragon species helps the riders in critical moments.

Ruffnut & Tuffnut Thorston

These mischievous twins share the two-headed Hideous Zippleback named Barf and Belch. Their chaotic nature often causes trouble, but they are fiercely loyal to their friends.
