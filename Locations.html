Iconic Locations in the Dragon Universe

Explore the rich and diverse locations featured in the How to Train Your Dragon movies and TV series. Each location is filled with history, mystery, and adventure.

Berk

Berk is a rugged island located on the Barbaric Archipelago. It is the home of Hiccup and the Vikings who have learned to coexist with dragons.

  • Features wooden Viking huts and the Great Hall.
  • Surrounded by cliffs and the icy sea.
  • Hub for dragon training and exploration.

Dragon's Edge

Dragon's Edge is a hidden outpost where Hiccup and his friends conduct research and protect dragons from poachers.

  • Located on a remote island with unique structures.
  • Serves as a strategic point for defending dragons.
  • Home to various dragon species and inventions.

The Hidden World

The Hidden World is a secret utopia beneath the sea where dragons live in harmony away from humans.

  • Filled with bioluminescent flora and fauna.
  • Serves as a sanctuary for endangered dragons.
  • Guarded by Toothless as the Alpha Dragon.
